Cognitive
distortion
in programming

As developers, we often face challenges that go against our productivity. But we often ignore the big picture. Some of these problems are subtle, some are significant. Sometimes they can be dealt with, and sometimes, unfortunately, not.

Together, they form something of a vicious cycle, and this can lead to long-term loss of productivity, bugs, and endless frustration.

If we can minimize the impact of one or more of these factors, then we can break the cycle and neutralize the rest.
